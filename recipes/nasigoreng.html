 Nasi Goreng

In Indonesian and Malay, nasi goreng is literally translated as "fried rice," and that is exactly what it is! The major ingredient is rice, and the only vegetables are onion and a small amount of beef. The sauce, which is created with kecap manis, a sweet soy sauce that darkens the rice when it cooks and caramelizes it, is what sets it apart from other Fried Rice meals.
